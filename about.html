AspeQt, Atari Serial Peripheral Emulator for Qt

Summary
AspeQt-2k25 emulates Atari SIO peripherals when connected to an Atari 8-bit computer with an SIO2PC cable. In that respect t's similar to programs like APE, Atari810, and AspeQt. The main difference is that it's free (unlike APE and AspeQt) and it's cross-platform (unlike Atari810 and APE).
